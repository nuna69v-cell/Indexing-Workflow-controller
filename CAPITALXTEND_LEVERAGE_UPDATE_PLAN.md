# CapitalXtend Leverage Update Plan

## Event Overview
CapitalXtend is launching Unlimited Leverage on selected instruments, providing traders with greater flexibility and improved capital efficiency.

## Schedule
**Date:** March 16, 2026 (16.03.2026)
**Time:** 14:00 (GMT+3)

## Affected Instruments
### FX Majors
* EURUSD
* AUDUSD
* GBPUSD
* USDJPY
* USDCAD
* USDCHF

### Spot Metals
* XAUUSD (Gold)
* XAGUSD (Silver)

## Conditions
* **Volume Band:** Unlimited Leverage applies to the first volume band (0.00 – 0.10 lots).
* **Benefit:** Allows smaller positions to be opened with minimal margin requirements.

## Action Plan for the Trading System
1. **Pre-Update Check (Before 16.03.2026 14:00 GMT+3)**
   - Ensure all open positions on the affected instruments are closed, as leverage settings cannot be changed while positions are open.
   - Pause automated trading (EAs) on these instruments right before the update.

2. **Update Leverage Setting (16.03.2026 14:00 GMT+3)**
   - Log in to the CapitalXtend Client Area.
   - Navigate to **Manage Account**.
   - Select the trading account.
   - Click **Change Leverage**.
   - Select **Unlimited**.

3. **Post-Update Check**
   - Verify the leverage change in the account settings.
   - Update the trading system's internal risk management and margin calculation logic to account for the new unlimited leverage on the 0.00-0.10 lot tier.
   - Resume automated trading.
